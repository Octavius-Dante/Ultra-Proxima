Rejection Notification Forms

Create and configure Order Rejection Notification Form

In this step, you will send out a notification form if the order is rejected.

  1. Click on the (+) after the Reject node.

  2. Click on Form >Blank Form.

  3. Create Form Pop-Up will appear

    • Enter Order Rejection Form in the Name field.
    • Click on Create button.

  4. Select Order Rejection Form and click on Open Editor.

  5. Design the form by dragging and dropping the corresponding Form elements as shown below.

    Field type Field Value Configuration (Read Only)
    Headline 1 Rejected Sales Order
    Paragraph The Order has been rejected. The Order details are:
    Text Material X
    Number Quantity X
    Text Customer Name X
    Date Expected Delivery Date X
    Text Area Message to Buyer X

  6. Click Save

  7. After the Form design completed go back to the process select the Order Rejection Form

  8. Click General tab

  9. In the Subject field:

    • type Your order has been rejected for
    • click to map material from Process Content => salesorderdetails

  10. In Recipients under Users, enter the email address as ac096819u21@sapexperienceacademy.com

  1. Save your work

  2. Configure the inputs of Order Rejection Form. Navigate to Inputs and map the fields accordingly.

  1. Save the form.
